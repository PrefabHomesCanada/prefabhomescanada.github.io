Quick summary

We don’t sell personal info

We do not sell your personal information. We use analytics to understand site performance and improve content.

Affiliate links may earn a commission

If you click an affiliate link and buy something, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Most data is standard web log data

Like most sites, we may receive IP address, browser type, pages visited, and approximate location (city/region).

You can control cookies

You can limit cookies via your browser settings. Some analytics may still collect aggregated data.

Information we collect

The site may collect information in the following ways, depending on how it is configured and how you interact with it.

Information collected automatically

  • IP address (often used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate location)
  • Browser/device type, operating system, and referring pages
  • Pages visited, time on page, clicks, and general usage patterns

Information you provide (only if enabled)

  • If the site provides a direct contact method (for example, an email address), and you choose to use it, you may provide message content and identifying details.
  • If no direct contact method is listed, this type of collection may not apply.

We aim to collect only what we need to operate and improve the site. If a feature is not enabled, then the related collection may not apply.

Cookies & analytics

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We may use cookies or similar technologies to support site functionality and understand performance.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to measure traffic and improve content. Analytics may collect usage data such as page views, links clicked, approximate location, and device information.

Cookie controls

  • You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings.
  • Blocking cookies can affect site features (depending on what’s used).
  • Some analytics may still measure aggregated events using non-cookie methods (depending on configuration).

If you want a stricter setup later (consent banners / Consent Mode), you can add it, but this policy already explains the purpose and categories.

Affiliate links

PrefabHomesCanada may include affiliate links (for example, to Amazon). When you click an affiliate link, a third party may use cookies or similar technologies to track referrals and attribute purchases.

Affiliate programs are operated by third parties. Their data practices are governed by their own policies, not ours. For more details, see our Disclosure.

Sharing

We may share information in limited situations:

Service providers

We may use service providers (hosting, analytics, measurement tools) who process information on our behalf to operate the site. They are expected to protect data and use it only for the service provided.

Legal and safety

We may disclose information if required by law, to respond to lawful requests, or to protect rights, safety, and site integrity.

Your choices

Browser controls

  • Block or delete cookies using your browser settings.
  • Use private browsing modes to reduce stored data on your device.

Analytics choices

You can reduce analytics collection by using browser privacy controls, blocking third-party cookies, or using privacy-focused browser settings. Some aggregated measurement may still occur depending on configuration.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please avoid sending sensitive personal information through basic channels.

Children

PrefabHomesCanada is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? PrefabHomesCanada may not provide direct individual support. If a direct contact method is listed on the site, you may use it for policy-related questions.

You can always manage cookies and tracking through your browser settings, regardless of whether a direct contact method is available.
