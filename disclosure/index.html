Verification Responsibility

Before purchasing any prefab home or related product, you should:

  • Confirm zoning and occupancy rules with your municipality
  • Request documentation from the seller (drawings, certifications, specs)

  • Confirm insulation levels and climate suitability
  • Understand delivery, foundation, and installation requirements
