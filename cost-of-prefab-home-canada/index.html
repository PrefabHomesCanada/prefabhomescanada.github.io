Cost of a Prefab Home in Canada (2026)

Most “prefab home price” conversations start and end with the listing price. In Canada, that’s rarely the real budget. This page explains the installed-cost drivers: delivery and offload, foundations, permits, utilities, plumbing/electrical, insulation upgrades, and winterization. Use it to plan realistically and avoid expensive surprises.
